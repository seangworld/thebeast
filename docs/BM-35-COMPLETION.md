# BM-35 Completion Evidence

BM-35 provides deterministic retirement income timelines, year-by-year income and expense projections, gap analysis, comparison of up to three saved scenarios, confidence indicators, printable reporting, JSON export, explanation, and financial education.

## Canonical Data and Safety

The displayed timeline, comparison, report, and export use only the last successfully persisted BM-34 assumptions. Each saved timeline stores its scenario snapshot, calculation version, and reproducibility key. Provenance remains attached to values, and the product does not provide personalized investment, withdrawal, retirement-date, benefit-claiming, tax, borrowing, or debt-payoff advice.
